                  Types Of Vacuums

                  Central Vacuums

                  The best central vacuum systems are generally installed in a garage, basement, or storage room. From the built in vacuum, pipes are then connected throughout the whole house and inlets are installed at each port of the pipes. These inlets connect to hoses and other central vacuum accessories to clean dirt, dust particles, allergens, etc. from that specific room. We offer a variety of Central Vacuums to suit your specific household or business need.

                  Shop Central Vacuum

                  Household Vacuums

                  We are not just one of the best known central vacuum and whole house vacuum retailers, we also offer a variety of household vacuums, upright vacuums, canister vacuums, and household vacuum accessories. A household vacuum is the typical vacuum you usually see and grew up with. They consist of upright vacuums and canister vacuums for your every day cleaning needs, Miele and SEBO are widely regarded as the leading manufacturers in the industry.

                  Shop Household Vacuum

                  Commercial Vacuums

                  A commercial vacuum is built for commercial and industrial vacuum needs such as for Hotels, and Restaurants. Commercial vacuums typically have the same power as a central vacuum, but it's either on your back such as a Backpack Vacuum or you push/pull like a regular vacuum cleaner. Browse our immense inventory of commercial vacuums, we carry canister vacuums, upright vacuums, air movers, carpet extractors, floor machines, and have all the accessories you need.
